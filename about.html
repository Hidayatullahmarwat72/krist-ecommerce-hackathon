About Krist

Krist is a modern e-commerce platform built during a 5-hour hackathon. Our mission is to provide a clean, responsive shopping experience using only HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript.

Features include user authentication, product browsing, cart and wishlist management, dark mode, and more—all stored locally with LocalStorage.
